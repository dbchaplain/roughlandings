Rough Landings Day 5: You are Part of Something Bigger
Sometimes everything is overwhelming. All of these wonderful students around, amazing faculty, and caring staff. Sometimes I just feel like one of many. Like I'm just a number trying to be statistically significant. Everyone around me is smart, and my anxiety eats away at me as I try to remember that I'm smart, too. That I have talents and I am part of something bigger.

Tao Te Ching - Lao Tzu - chapter 11
Thirty spokes share the wheel's hub;
It is the center hole that makes it useful.
Shape clay into a vessel;
It is the space within that makes it useful.
Cut doors and windows for a room;
It is the holes which make it useful.
Therefore profit comes from what is there;
Usefulness from what is not there.
